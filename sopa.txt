Ingredients:
- 2 turnip heads with greens;
- 2 medium potatoes;
- 1 medium onion;
- Olive oil, 2 tablespoons;
- Salt, 1 teaspoon.

Preparation:
Wash the turnips well and separate the heads from the greens.
Peel the turnips and cut into cubes.
Cut the greens into julienne strips and set aside.
Place the potatoes,
the onion and the turnip heads in a pot over heat,
with a little water.
Let it cook.
Using a hand blender, reduce everything to a puree.
Add a little more water if necessary.
Drizzle the turnip soup with olive oil,
season with salt and add the turnip greens,
previously cut.
Bring to a boil and cook for about 10 minutes on low heat.
Remove from heat and serve immediately.
